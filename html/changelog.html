01 October 2020

Floof Ball#0798 Kathrin Morrison updated:

  • Boxstation Bridge - Buttons and status displays are no longer under tables/window grilles.

28 September 2020

NotRanged updated:

  • Drones can now speak in :b for binary channel. They can also receive (but not transmit) engineering and common channels.
  • Added 'E' hotkey quick equip functionality to drones.
  • Drones can now use vendors. Engi-vend etc are free, any item that does have a cost will subtract it from the engineering budget.
  • Drones can now understand common.
  • Dronechat channel moved to :r, for 'repair'.

27 September 2020

Floof Ball#0798 Kathrin Morrison updated:

  • New sprites for improvised shotgun and rifle.
  • Revolving rifles - three, including a craftable and a golden one.
  • Improvised shotgun refactored - it's now a break-action shotgun; the barrel must be opened and closed manually.
  • .32 rubber bullets now do 24 stamina damage from 20.
  • Improvised laser is now worth crafting. It's now a burst-fire weapon that does 7 damage. per laser, it fires in bursts of three. It can fire 25 lasers, meaning 8 bursts and a single laser.

KathrinBailey updated:

  • A completely new prison for Boxstation!
  • Boxstation Security has received an aesthetic overhaul.
  • Huge heaps of mapping errors in Boxstation Security have been fixed.

NotRanged updated:

  • Fusion max temperature clamped to 5e9 K.
  • Sonic jackhammer now takes 25 seconds to destroy a reinforced wall. Added a 5 second timer to destroying regular walls.
  • Adminspawn supersonic jackhammer.
